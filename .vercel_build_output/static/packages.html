Frequently asked questions

As much as you want to be. We have some clients who are very involved but most let our team take the ball and run with it.
Am I under any contract?
Our monthly plans are flexible and do not include a contract. Our annual plans can save you money but are non-refundable.
How do I know you’ll do a good job for me?
Check out our reviews - we go above and beyond for our clients, and our reviews reflect this.
What if I already have a website or domain through another provider?
It’s a common misconception that if you already have a domain or a website you are stuck with your current provider.

This couldn’t be further from the truth. If you have an existing website through another provider, you will keep the same website address.

When it comes time to set your site live, our technical team will handle everything - you may need to approve your current provider to grant us access.
